Pele

The man who changed football forever

  • 1940 - Born in Edson Arantes do Nascimento, Brazil.
  • 1956 - Joins Santos Fc. at age 15, scores in his debut match
  • 1958 - Becomes the youngest player to score win a world cup at age 17 in Sweeden
  • 1962 - Wins his second world cup in Chile, while having an injury
  • 1970 - Helps Brazil to a third world cup victory in Mexico
  • 1975 - Signs with the new york cosmos, bringing soccer to the American mainstream
  • 1977 - Plays his final professional match and officially retires from the sport.
  • 1999 - Named Athelete of the century by the international olympic committee
  • 2022 - Died on December 29, 2022, leaving a legacy of 1000+ goals
