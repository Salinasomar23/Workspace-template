Design Decisions:

For the design of the field guide, I wanted to make a one piece themed design for the field guide. The colors I chose were based on somewhat of the color scheme from the One Piece series, which has a soft blue background and light brownish color boxes for the content areas and a white sidebar. Fonnts used in this field guide are Sansita and Inria Sans. Sansita is used for the headings and inra Sans is used for the body text.I also wanted to include different types of images so it can pair up the witht the text to make it more engaging. I wanted to go with this design because its one of my favorite shows and i wanted to create a field guide about the fruits beacuse they have cool and unique powers.

Technical Challenges

Some of the challenges I faced when building the field guide was trying to make the mobile version work properly and making sure the layout didnt break or mess up. What I did to solve the problems I was facing was watching youtube videos and a bit of AI help to help me with some parts of the code to make it work better. Another challenge I was facing was trying to make my 3rd topics image be in triangle shape like the original design I had but couldnt do it. What I did to solve that problem was to make them stack vertically instead. I thought it came out better that way on desktop view and mobile view.

Responsive Strategy

Adapting my design from desktop to mobile I used media queries to make sure that the layout would change properly like making the breakpoint to 640p and making the sidebar navigation become a hamburger menu when the screen starts to shrink below 640px. I also adjusted the images to fit the screen perfectly instead of them taking up the whole screen. For example the fruits were oranigzed vertically in columns and the rectangular images were made in rows.

Learning Moments

What I learned about flexbox is that they can make things easier like centeriong or aligning, create coloumns and rows, and sizing. My favorite thing about flexbox is sizing becauses it made it easy to control the size of elements and make them responsive to different screen sizes.

Future Improvements

Future improvements I would like to make to the field guide is to add more interactive options like being able to click on the images to get a better look at the for both desktop and mobile views. Another thing would want to change is the mobile layout as I felt like I could make it more better and fluid.
